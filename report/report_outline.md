# Report Outline

## 1. Executive Summary

Briefly state the main growth or operations lever, the estimated opportunity, and the proposed workflow.

## 2. Evidence Collected

Summarize funnel walkthrough, review themes, and benchmark research.

## 3. Funnel Diagnostic

Map evidence to acquisition, activation, retention, revenue, and referral stages.

## 4. Opportunity Ranking

Use an ICE or RICE table to compare possible interventions.

## 5. Model Summary

Show the headline result from the spreadsheet model.

## 6. Prototype Summary

Explain what the AI-assisted workflow does and where it fits.

## 7. 30-Day Execution Plan

Explain how this could be tested with a limited cohort.

## 8. Limitations

State evidence gaps, proxy assumptions, and safety boundaries.
