# Project Scope

## Working Lever

Intake-to-activation.

## Why This Lever

This lever is the most defensible because it can be examined using public and first-hand evidence. The intake flow can be walked directly, screenshots can be documented, review themes can be coded, and the business impact of improving conversion can be modeled with transparent assumptions.

## Project Theme

Legion Intake-to-Activation Audit + AI Pre-Intake Workflow

## Hypothesis

Prospective patients may face avoidable friction before completing intake or reaching the first visit. A lightweight AI-assisted pre-intake workflow may reduce confusion, clarify next steps, and improve activation without requiring major engineering lift.

## Evidence Standards

* Funnel observations are structural evidence, not proof of drop-off.
* Review themes are directional, not statistically representative.
* Benchmarks are external proxies and must be labeled clearly.
* All model inputs must be cited or labeled as assumptions.
