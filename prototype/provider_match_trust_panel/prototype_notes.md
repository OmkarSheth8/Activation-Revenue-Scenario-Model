# Prototype Notes

## Final design decision

The Streamlit prototype was simplified into an interactive version of the Excel activation revenue model.

The slide deck will explain the full project story. The Streamlit app only supports the model by letting users test assumptions interactively.

## What problem this solves

Reviewers do not need to edit the Excel workbook manually to test different assumptions. They can adjust intake volume, funnel rates, revenue assumptions, and improvement scenarios directly in the app.

## What this demonstrates

* Funnel modeling
* Scenario sensitivity analysis
* Revenue impact estimation
* Clear separation between sample assumptions and actual company data

## What it does not demonstrate

* Real Legion internal performance
* Real revenue forecast
* Real patient data
* Clinical decision support
* Provider matching
* Insurance verification

## How to improve accuracy

Replace sample assumptions with internal funnel analytics, actual visit completion rates, actual reimbursement data, and actual follow-up retention data.
