# Model Plan

The model will estimate the potential business impact of improving intake-to-first-visit conversion.

## Planned Tabs

1. Inputs
2. Funnel Model
3. Scenarios
4. Sensitivity

## Key Inputs

* Monthly intake starts
* Intake completion rate
* Completed intake to first-visit rate
* First-visit revenue assumption
* Follow-up visit rate
* Follow-up revenue assumption
* Conversion lift from intervention

## Output Metrics

* Incremental completed intakes
* Incremental first visits
* Incremental follow-up visits
* Estimated incremental revenue
